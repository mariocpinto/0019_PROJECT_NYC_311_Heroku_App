NYC 311 Call Data Analysis

This page presents the results of the analysis of NYC 311 data from 2010 - 2015.

Approximately 74% of the calls in this period fall into five categories:

  • Heating and Electric:
    Calls with complaint type 'Heating', 'Heat/Hot Water', and 'Electric'.

  • Plumbing and Construction:
    Calls with complaint type 'Plumbing', 'General Construction', 'Paint - Plaster', 'Nonconst', 'General Construction/Plumbing', and 'Building/Use'.

  • Streets, Traffic and Parking:
    Calls with complaint type 'Street Light Condition', 'Street Condition', 'Blocked Driveway', 'Illegal Parking', 'Traffic Signal Condition', 'Damaged Tree', and 'Broken Muni Meter'.

  • Water and Hygiene:
    Calls with complaint type 'Water System', 'Sewer', 'Dirty Conditions', 'Sanitation Condition', 'Unsanitary Condition', and 'Rodent'.

  • Noise:
    Calls with complaint type 'Noise', 'Noise - Commercial', and 'Noise - Street/Sidewalk'.
